About BiteVerse

Biteverse started as a inspired by movie and to have a great street food. We wanted to make food that is quick, tasty, and made with fresh ingredients so everybody can swing by between shifts and munch like a hero.

We serve a mix of classic street favorites with our own twists — fusion wraps, loaded Fries and burgers,shakes. Our food truck appears at festivals, college campuses, and community events.

What We Value

  • Fresh ingredients — we never compromise.
  • Quick service — because heroes are busy.
